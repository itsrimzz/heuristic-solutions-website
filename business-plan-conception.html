Business Plan Conception

Business Plan is the future of your business. An investor will be highly interested in knowing your business plan because they are highly interested in ROI. This is the reason why your business plan has to be strategic and spot on. There are some very important and key elements of this plan. With an effective business plan you can grab an opportunity which can take your business to a whole new level. The business plan for your organization should depict a written strategy from a marketing, financial and operational viewpoint.

The plan includes overall budget, current and projected financing, a market analysis and its marketing strategy approach. As a business owner you will be required to project the expenses and revenue in stipulated time frame. You have to also define the operational cost which will be related to your business.

There are 7 key elements or stages in a business plan:

1. Executive Summary: It is a summary and a collection of details about what you want and is very important from reader per-se. Within the executive summary you should include points following points

  • a) Concept of Business
  • b) Finances
  • c) Requirement of Funds
  • d) Current Status of your business
  • e) What have you achieved

2. Business Description: Here you need to talk about the industry where you are located and how you a cut above the rest. Describe the current scenarios and what the future possibilities are as per growth. You need to make sure that you are talking about the market that you are dealing; how will the growth of market and new innovations can make or break your business.

3. Marketing Strategy: An effective market analysis will help you understand the market, the competitors and whether your product is following a rat race or is unique. An effective marketing strategy is the key here. Market analysis will help an entrepreneur to fix the price of the product, the distributional and promotional strategies. This can help the business to move towards profitability. Analysis will help in understanding the growth in the industry and will also help you in understanding the future of your product.

4. Competitive Analysis: The whole idea of doing the competitive analysis is to understand the strength and weakness persisting in your market. This is a strategy which will give you a competitive advantage as you will be aware of the strength and weakness of your competitors.

5. Design and Development Plan: This section of your business plan speaks about the design of the product, define the development with the reference of production, marketing and formulating new budget which will help your company reach the desired goal. You need to include subjects such as Product Development, Market Development and Organizational Development.

6. Operations and Management Plan: This segment will depict how you are planning to move your business ahead. So basically this section will define the roles and responsibilities of people who are associated with you. It is one of the most important parts of a business plan. An investor will be really keen to know the structure of your organization.

7. Financials: Financials will help in running your business smoothly. This section is extremely important from an investor point of view. The financial statement portrays the actual value of your company. The financial statement will include an income statement and a balance sheet.
