This gives you a clear idea of what is working and what is not working for your brand. However digital marketing is vast and it includes:

Social Media

This is the widest part of digital marketing because social media itself is huge and the number of users is growing day on day basis. For example there are over 200 million Twitter users, over 1 billion Facebook users. Posting regular and quality content on these social media can help increase you reach manifold as it increases visibility amongst your targeted audience. SEO

Creating your website and social media pages and the promoting your content in those platforms is not everything. Search engines have improved their algorithms and in a way it is favoring websites. SEO is extremely important and a proper SEO strategy can work wonders for your website. A strategized SEO technique can improve the ranking of a website. Heuristic Solutions can help you with a strategized approach to a proper SEO and this will include:

  • Keyword Research: We will closely monitor various keywords and how many searches are there for each of them. Thereafter we will be choosing the right keyword which will be in sync with your business.
  • On-Page SEO: This helps in search engines locate your site. So we will be making some small changes to your site which includes optimizing page titles, headers, descriptions, image names and alt tags, page content, and URLs.
  • Off-Page SEO: This is backlinking your site/pages from other sites. This helps search engines to determine that your website is a reliable source of information.

SEM

Search Engine Marketing increases the visibility of your brand with the help of sponsored links on search engines. SEO and SEM together can work wonders for you. Just like the SEO our SEM process also starts with keyword research. Once we have found the right set of keywords we will need an ad which will compel the customers to click on. Landing Pages play an important role here so we will discuss with you and create some amazing landing pages which will lead to conversion. Once the campaign is created it will be our responsibility to manage it for you.

Email Marketing

If used effectively email marketing is extremely beneficial. The reason why it is beneficial is because every day people not only check their social media accounts but also their email. Email Marketing is one of the most inexpensive types of marketing strategy and can be a part of any type of marketing plan. Email Newsletters are spectacular tool to communicate with your existing customers and win over new customers. We at Heuristic Solutions will design effective newsletters such as designing and building custom HTML templates to monitoring as to how many times you should be sending the newsletters.
